## questions
- AC²-VLA's depth dimension: transformer layer depth (→ dynamic layer skipping, similar to MoLe-VLA) or 3D scene depth (→ depth-conditioned spatial computation, similar to DepthCache)? The mechanism differs significantly and determines which prior surf work is the closest comparison.
- ReconVLA's reconstruction objective: MAE-style masked prediction (task-agnostic) or action-predictive reconstruction (task-conditioned)? MAE-style doesn't address action relevance; action-predictive is closer to DynaFLIP and becomes a strong baseline for [[eval/reconstruction]].
- PosPrune's center-bias correction: does correcting architectural center bias expose genuine content-based token importance, or overcorrect in scenes where the task-relevant object is genuinely at center? The correction could suppress true signal in center-weighted manipulation setups.
- Carried from 5/31: VLA-ATTC critic training (joint vs. separate, calibration dependency); ElegantVLA conditioning signal (visual complexity vs. uncertainty vs. instruction specificity); GuidedVLA attention scope (image-level vs. patch-level); GeoWeaver vs. DynaFLIP (orthogonal geometry + dynamics axes, or competing claims about what makes a feature action-relevant).

## risks
- **Gaze on the Prize Day 7 unread.** [[experiments/contrastive alignment]] cannot be designed. Tianqin conversation has no framing.
- **Efficiency/RL module formulation now covers four distinct approaches.** AC²-VLA (spatial/depth redundancy) + ElegantVLA (temporal mode switch) + VLA-ATTC (critic-based confidence gating) + BRPO (budget-relative RL reward). These are not interchangeable; picking one without reading all four is a design risk.
- **ReconVLA vs. surf's eval/reconstruction.** Reconstruction-guided attention grounding is an existing published approach to fixing VLA attention dispersion. If [[eval/reconstruction]] measures reconstruction quality rather than structural prior quality, it doesn't distinguish surf's approach from ReconVLA's. The metric design needs to answer: what does the prior add over reconstruction supervision?
- **PosPrune's encoder-architectural center bias.** If the vision encoder's CLS attention intrinsically encodes center bias, surf's line-guided selection vs. center-bias baseline comparison is confounded by encoder architecture, not just scene composition. Baseline design needs to control for this.
