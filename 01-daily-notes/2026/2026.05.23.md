## papers

### digest (auto)

**core**
- [GesVLA: Gesture-Aware Vision-Language-Action Model Embedded Representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22812v1) — arXiv 2026-05-21
  Extends VLA conditioning beyond text to gestures. Useful as a contrast point for [[claims/language helps]]: if non-language conditioning also selects task-relevant regions, the language-vs-zero-language ablation matters more than the language-vs-no-conditioning one.
- [Swift Sampling: Selecting Temporal Surprises via Taylor Series](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22678v1) — arXiv 2026-05-21
  Selective temporal sampling driven by prediction-residual surprise. Direct conceptual neighbor to [[claims/iterative selection]] and [[concepts/visual budget]] — does residual-driven selection beat learned scorers at matched compute?
- [ReconVLA: Reconstructive Vision-Language-Action Model as Effective Robot Perceiver](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/c87ea2afcd9212e5128e01daf17c733d285ac345) — Song et al. · 2025
  Tests the surf hypothesis: VLAs can be trained to allocate visual attention via a reconstructive grounding signal. Compare against [[eval/reconstruction]] — does their grounding loss avoid the MAE-artifact failure mode flagged in [[questions]]?

**adjacent**
- [From Abstraction to Instantiation: Learning Behavioral Representation for Vision-Language-Action Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22671v1) — arXiv 2026-05-21
  VLA generalization under distribution shift via behavior representations. Relevant if surf extends to action-task eval — frames what "transferable" prior/selector outputs would look like.
- [DecQ: Detail-Condensing Queries for Enhanced Reconstruction and Generation in Representation Autoencoders](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22777v1) — arXiv 2026-05-21
  Frozen VFM tokens + learned detail-condensing queries. Worth scanning for an alternative to MAE-style reconstruction loss in [[eval/reconstruction]].
- [SmolVLA: A Vision-Language-Action Model for Affordable and Efficient Robotics](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/6ab4d113676d00e74b55e918fee4c7affaa8652f) — Shukor et al. · 2025
  Small efficient VLA with async inference. Compute-matched VLA reference point, not a method to adopt.

**horizon**
- [Slimmable ConvNeXt: Width-Adaptive Inference for Efficient Multi-Device Deployment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22677v1) — arXiv 2026-05-21
  Width-adaptive inference under fluctuating compute. Tangential, but the "compute budget that changes at runtime" framing is closer to surf's adaptive-compute story than typical static-budget work.
- [Learnings from Scaling Visual Tokenizers for Reconstruction and Generation](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/2eb5774bbadc7790afd2858667d31238336ff423) — Hansen-Estruch et al. · 2025
  Reconstruction-vs-generation tradeoff for visual tokenizers — directly informs [[eval/reconstruction]] design choices.
