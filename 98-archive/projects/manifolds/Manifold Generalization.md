## Manifold Generalization; A Definition

**Definition 1** *(Cue Invariance)*: The ability of a visual system to recognize an object's form or shape regardless of the specific visual cues that define it, as introduced in [@massotCueInvariantGeometricStructure2023].

**Definition 2** *(Manifold Generalization)*: The ability of the manifolds in a specific layer to generalize across *cues*.
