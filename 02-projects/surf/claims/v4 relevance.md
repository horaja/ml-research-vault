# v4 relevance

## claim
V4-derived feature preferences may provide useful mid-level shape guidance for selective visual computation.

## caution
This is a biological motivation and design hypothesis, not evidence that the model is biologically faithful.

## needed evidence
- Operational definition of V4 alignment for action tasks.
- Comparison against line drawings, Canny edges, and learned mid-level features.

## baseline that could kill it
- Generic structural baselines match or beat V4-derived scoring.
- V4-derived scoring improves gaze similarity but not task performance.
